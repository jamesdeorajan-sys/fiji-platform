Regions explained

Where in Fiji should you stay?

Denarau Island
10 minutes from Nadi Airport. Fiji's resort hub — Hilton, Sheraton, Westin, Sofitel, Radisson all here. Port Denarau Marina for island day trips. Best for: convenience, families, first-time visitors.
Best partner: The Palms Denarau
Coral Coast
45–90 mins south of Nadi. InterContinental Natadola, Outrigger, Shangri-La, Warwick. Beautiful beaches, more authentic Fiji feel than Denarau. Best for: couples, longer stays, culture.
Transfer from Nadi: FJD $129–299
Mamanuca Islands
30–90 mins by ferry from Port Denarau. Castaway, Tokoriki, Malolo. Protected lagoons, white sand, snorkelling. Best for: couples, short breaks, island hopping.
Ferry from Port Denarau Marina
Yasawa Islands
4.5 hours by catamaran from Port Denarau. Blue Lagoon Beach Resort, Mantaray Island, Navutu Stars. Remote, pristine, world-class diving. Best for: honeymooners, divers, adventurers.
Best partner: Blue Lagoon Beach Resort
Pacific Harbour
2 hours from Nadi. Known as Fiji's adventure capital — shark diving, whitewater rafting, zip-lining. Pearl South Pacific resort. Best for: adrenaline seekers.
Transfer from Nadi: FJD $269+
Taveuni
Fiji's garden island — the Rainbow Reef dive site is one of the world's best. Remote, lush, authentic. Requires domestic flight from Nadi. Best for: serious divers and nature lovers.
Domestic flight from Nadi

Fiji accommodation — frequently asked questions

  • What is the best resort in Fiji for families with young kids?+
    The Palms Denarau is the top choice for families with young children. Its spacious self-contained villas with full kitchens let families self-cater, the Denarau beach is calm and safe for swimming, and Port Denarau Marina is minutes away for island day trips. It's also just 10 minutes from Nadi Airport — ideal if you have tired children on arrival. Contact reservations@thepalmsdenarau.com or WhatsApp +679 675 0104.
  • What is the best resort in Fiji for a honeymoon?+
    Blue Lagoon Beach Resort on Nacula Island in the Yasawa Islands is Fiji's premier honeymoon destination. Remote, private, and surrounded by world-class reef. Getting there: transfer from Nadi Airport to Port Denarau Marina (FJD $55 sedan), then the Blue Lagoon Cruises catamaran takes approximately 4.5 hours. WhatsApp +679 776 6223.
  • What does a week in Fiji cost for 2 adults?+
    Budget: The Palms Denarau approximately AU$2,500–3,500 per week. Mid-range Coral Coast resort: AU$4,000–6,000. Luxury remote island (Blue Lagoon): AU$6,000–12,000 including catamaran transfers. July/August school holidays are peak pricing — book 3+ months ahead.
  • What makes Blue Lagoon Beach Resort special?+
    Blue Lagoon Beach Resort offers a combination that no other Fiji resort matches: genuine remote island seclusion, world-class PADI diving on pristine Yasawa reef, authentic Fijian village experiences, and all-inclusive packages that cover meals, activities, and non-motorised water sports. The Yasawa Islands are significantly drier than the main island. The resort is 100% Fijian-owned.
  • Where should a family of 4 stay with a budget of AU$8,000?+
    The Palms Denarau is the best value. A spacious 2-bedroom villa accommodates the family, full kitchen saves on dining costs, and the Denarau location gives easy access to day trips and the beach. Budget breakdown: accommodation AU$5,000–6,000 for 7 nights, transfers AU$200–400, activities and dining AU$1,500–2,000. Contact reservations@thepalmsdenarau.com for exact rates.
  • What is the best time to visit Fiji resorts?+
    June to September is Fiji's dry season — the best time for most resorts. July and August are peak with best weather but highest prices. May, June, September, and October offer excellent conditions with fewer crowds and lower rates. The Yasawa Islands are drier than the Coral Coast year-round. Avoid January to March — cyclone season, though prices are lowest.
  • What is the check-in time at Fiji resorts?+
    Most Fiji resorts check in at 3:00pm and check out at 11:00am. The Palms Denarau checks in at 3:00pm. Early check-in is often available on request — always ask when booking, especially if arriving on an early morning flight from Australia or New Zealand.
  • Can I do island day trips from Denarau resorts?+
    Yes — Port Denarau Marina is the departure point for all Mamanuca and Yasawa day trips. From The Palms Denarau the marina is 5 minutes away. Day trips to Castaway, Monuriki (Cast Away film island), South Sea Island, and Beachcomber depart daily from AU$120pp. Full-day Yasawa trips also available. Nadi Airport Transfers can take you to the marina for FJD $55 sedan.

Information accurate as of May 2026. Always confirm current rates directly with resorts.
