Why the Mamanuca Islands?

The Mamanuca Islands are Fiji's most visited island group — for good reason. Sitting 20–50 kilometres west of Nadi, they combine spectacular scenery with genuine accessibility. You can be on an island beach 30 minutes after leaving your Denarau hotel.

The Mamanucas also have an unexpectedly diverse range of experiences: ultra-luxury private island resorts, budget backpacker operations, the famous Cloud 9 floating platform, surf breaks on the outer reef, and world-class snorkelling that rivals anywhere in the Pacific.

Film location: The Mamanucas featured as the setting for the reality TV show Survivor: Fiji and the 2000 Tom Hanks film Cast Away, both filmed on Monuriki Island. The island is now a popular day trip from Mana Island.

Surfing in the Mamanucas

The Mamanuca reef breaks are among the most famous in the South Pacific. Cloudbreak and Restaurants (both off Tavarua Island) are two of the most celebrated left-handers in the world. Tavarua Island Resort has long hosted international surf competitions.

  • Cloudbreak: World-class left-hander. Breaks over shallow coral reef. Experienced surfers only. Accessible from Namotu and Tavarua islands.
  • Restaurants: A more forgiving left — long ride, powerful barrel. Better for intermediate surfers than Cloudbreak.
  • Swimming Pools: Beginner-friendly reef break accessible from Namotu Island Resort.
Surf season: The best swells arrive April–October. Peak surf season (June–August) can mean crowded lineups at Cloudbreak. The Mamanucas are internationally known in the surfing world — book surf-specific resorts well ahead.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Mamanuca Islands from Nadi?
Transfer from Nadi Airport to Port Denarau Marina (20–30 minutes), then take a ferry or speedboat. Multiple operators including South Sea Cruises run daily departures. Booking ferry tickets in advance is recommended in peak season.
What is the best Mamanuca island to visit for a day trip?
South Sea Island is the most popular — tiny, beautiful, and with the best accessible snorkelling in the group. For adults wanting a different experience, Cloud 9 (floating bar and restaurant) is unique. For families, Treasure Island or Bounty Island are solid choices.
What is Cloud 9 Fiji and how do I get there?
Cloud 9 is a floating pontoon platform in the Mamanucas with a bar, pizza restaurant, and small pool. It's adults-focused, popular for half-day and full-day trips. Boats depart from Port Denarau Marina. Book in advance — it sells out on weekends.
Is Castaway Island (Qalito) worth visiting?
Yes — Castaway Island Resort is one of Fiji's most consistently excellent resorts. Outstanding reef snorkelling, a top-rated dive operation, strong family facilities, and a genuine island feel just 45 minutes from Denarau. Day passes are available if you want to experience it without staying overnight.
Are there overwater bungalows in the Mamanuca Islands?
Yes — Likuliku Lagoon Resort on Malolo Island has some of Fiji's most famous overwater bures, with direct ocean access from private decks. Tokoriki and some other properties also offer premium overwater or semi-overwater options.
