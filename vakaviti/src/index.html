💡 Tip of the Day

🎯

Three ways to learn

Each mode is designed for a different stage of learning — from first encounter to long-term memory.

1

Quiz Mode Quiz

Tap the Quiz button in the header to start. You'll see an English meaning and must pick the correct Fijian word from 4 options — or flip to Fijian → English for a harder challenge.

  • Choose 5, 10, 20 or all 160 questions per round
  • Filter by category (e.g. Travel words only)
  • Earn XP for every correct answer and perfect round
  • Missed words are automatically sent to your Drill Bank
2

Flashcard Drill Drill

After a quiz, missed words go into your personal Drill Bank. The drill uses spaced repetition (SM-2) — harder words come back more often until they graduate out.

  • Launch from the quiz results screen
  • Each correct answer earns XP and advances the card
  • Graduated cards move to your Progress Dashboard
  • Cards have difficulty states: Learning → Due → Strong → Graduated
3

Progress Dashboard Dashboard

Tap the bar chart icon in the header to see your SM-2 card states across all 160 words — a stacked bar showing Unseen / Learning / Due / Review / Strong / Graduated.

  • Switch between English→Fijian and Fijian→English views
  • See how many words are due for review today
  • Track your mastery over time
