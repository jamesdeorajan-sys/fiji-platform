The route

Nadi Airport to Pearl South Pacific

From Nadi International Airport, the drive is approximately 145 km — about 2 hours 14 minutes via Queens Road. This is the longest paved-road transfer on Viti Levu before reaching Suva.

Practical notes for arrival

  • Drop-off is at the Pearl's lobby on the Pacific Harbour beachfront.
  • Pacific Harbour is the dive capital of Fiji — the Pearl arranges shark dive trips, river safari tours, and Beqa Lagoon ferries.
  • Because of the long drive, most travellers prefer Pacific Harbour as the start of their stay (fly in → transfer → relax) rather than the end.
  • For onward travel to Beqa Lagoon Resort, the boat departs from a private jetty 5 minutes from the Pearl — your driver can drop you directly there if requested.
