# Vision — Fiji Tour Transfers Platform

## North star

**To be the single most useful app a tourist has during their Fiji stay.**

Not "one of many booking sites." Not "a transfer company that also does tours." But the **first thing they save to their phone when they land**, and the **last app they message before they fly home**.

## Who we serve

### Primary customer
Australian, New Zealand, US and European tourists arriving at Nadi International Airport, ages 28–65, group size 1–8. They're often:
- First-time Fiji visitors who don't know which transfer to trust
- Family travellers with kids who need car seats and supermarket stops
- Honeymooners spending FJ$2,000+ on premium experiences
- Cruise ship passengers with 6–10 hours to fill at Lautoka or Port Denarau

### Secondary customer
Fiji-stay travellers who already have one transfer booked but want more during their stay:
- Tour add-ons (Cultural Night, Coral Coast horse riding, sand dunes)
- Concierge errands (pharmacy, supermarket, restaurant runs)
- Domestic flight transfers (Nausori connections to Savusavu, Taveuni)
- Departure transfer back to Nadi airport

## What we believe

**1. Mobile-first or die.** 87% of Fiji bookings happen on phones. The booking widget MUST be flawless on mobile before it's good on desktop.

**2. WhatsApp is the operating system of Fiji travel.** Customers don't want to download an app. They want to message us. Every customer touchpoint should funnel toward WhatsApp eventually.

**3. Trust is built in 60 seconds.** When a tourist lands at Nadi at 11pm with two crying kids, they don't have time to read reviews. The moment of trust is when our driver is **physically there with their name on a sign**. Our website's job is to make that moment feel inevitable.

**4. Price transparency is conversion.** Hidden fees kill bookings. Every quote shows distance, time, vehicle, taxes, discount, total — before they commit.

**5. The trip doesn't end at the airport.** Most transfer companies forget about you after the airport pickup. We are online for the entire stay. That's the differentiator.

**6. Brand consistency or you bleed trust on every visit.** Currently the live site has 4 different identities (fijitourtransfers.com / tourfiji.tours / Fiji Tourism Guide / fijitourstransfers.com). This must be fixed.

**7. AI-assisted, human-delivered.** AI calculates, recommends, schedules, and routes. A human being driver-bus-greets you at the airport and looks you in the eye. Both matter.

## What we don't do

- ❌ Compete on price alone (we're premium positioning)
- ❌ Take bookings we can't fulfil
- ❌ Pretend our locations are something they're not (Fiji Tourism Guide is OUR brand, not an independent reviewer)
- ❌ Build features customers don't ask for
- ❌ Sacrifice mobile UX for desktop polish

## 12-month vision

By April 2027, this platform should:

- ✅ Process 50+ direct bookings per week from the booking widget alone
- ✅ Have a live API connection to fijitourtransfers.com tour pricing (no manual sync)
- ✅ Accept Stripe payments directly (no WhatsApp handoff required for repeat customers)
- ✅ Have hotel-partner login for room-charging direct billing
- ✅ Power 5+ landing pages across the brand portfolio (coralcoasthorseriding.com, natadolabayhorseriding.com, etc)
- ✅ Have a returning-customer loyalty programme (saved profile, faster re-booking)
- ✅ Be referenced by name in Fiji Tourism trade publications as "the booking platform tourists actually use"

## What success looks like

A tourist in Sydney, planning a Fiji trip, hears about us from a friend. They visit fijitourtransfers.com, book their airport transfer in 90 seconds, save our WhatsApp on their phone. When they land in Fiji, our driver is there. During their stay, they message us 4 more times — pharmacy run, dinner reservation, Cultural Night Tour, return airport transfer. They tip generously, leave a 5-star Google review, refer 3 friends.

That whole journey should feel like one continuous, branded, trustworthy experience.

That's the win.
